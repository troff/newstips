  • Newstips is a news submission tool. Once you submit a news tip through this app, it will be added to the newswire on our website along with a 'verification score'. This score is based on your proximity to the event in question, and whether our reporters can independently verify the information you submit.

    In your news report, please stick to hard news information - 'who? what? when? where?' - provide as much information as you can, but please don't speculate on information you don't know.
